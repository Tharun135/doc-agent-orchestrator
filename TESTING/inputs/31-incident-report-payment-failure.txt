## Test Input 31: Incident Report (Payment Processing Failure)

**Incident ID:** INC-2041
**Severity:** P1
**Date:** Q1 2026

**Summary:**
Payments were rejected intermittently during peak hours. Errors were logged but not actioned. The payment gateway was restarted. Service was restored after restart. Several transactions were left in a pending state. Refunds were issued for failed transactions. The root cause is under investigation.

**Resolution steps taken:**
1. Gateway was restarted by on-call team.
2. Pending transactions were reprocessed.
3. Customers were notified via email.
4. Monitoring was increased.
