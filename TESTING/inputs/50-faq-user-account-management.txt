## Test Input 50: FAQ — User Account Management

**Source type:** Compiled from support ticket patterns

Q: How do I change my password?
A: Go to account settings and change it.

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Contact support. Takes some time.

Q: Can I have multiple accounts?
A: Depends on your plan.

Q: How do I transfer my account to someone else?
A: There's a process for that. Contact admin.

Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?
A: Data is retained for a while, then deleted.

Q: How do I add team members?
A: Admins can do this from the management area.
