## Test Input 51: Glossary — Industrial IoT Platform Terms

**Source type:** Whiteboard session notes

Key terms:

- Device: a physical thing that connects
- Connector: something that links devices to the platform
- Tag: a data point coming from a device
- Scan rate: how often data is collected
- Broker: handles message routing
- Payload: the data inside a message
- Gateway: sits between devices and the broker  
- Edge: the part of the system close to devices
- Protocol: how devices communicate (there are several)
- Subscription: how consumers get events

Some of these terms overlap with general IT concepts but mean specific things here.
